## The two axes of taktakk

Every learning module belongs to one of two axes:

**Shield** — protect life and safety.
Topics include water purification, first aid, safe routes, and device safety.

**Spear** — build capability and agency.
Topics include numeracy, communication, digital literacy, and logic.

## What taktakk looks like to an observer

Before you unlock it, taktakk looks like an ordinary clock app with alarm,
timer, and stopwatch functions. Nothing in the locked view reveals that any
learning content exists.
